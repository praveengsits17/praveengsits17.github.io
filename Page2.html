From Second Year:-

The most awaited moment came, we got a chance to attend IIT Bombay's cultural fest. The great thing about this fest is that it's Asia's largest cultural fest. There were so many events organised for the students. We enjoyed a stay of 4 days in Mumbai and celebrated new year with friends. After returning back from there, It was our turn to give the freshers party, and this time our juniors enjoyed the party and had fun....
